### **1. Home Page**
- **Purpose:** Provide an overview and quick access to main features.
- **Components:**
  - **Header:** App logo, navigation links, user profile icon.
  - **Main Features:** Quick links or buttons to Hotel Booking, Weather Check, To-Do Activities, etc.
  - **Search Bar:** To search for hotels, activities, or nearby places.
  - **Footer:** Contact info, privacy policy, terms of service.

### **2. Hotel Booking**
- **Purpose:** Allow users to search and book hotels.
- **Components:**
  - **Search Bar:** Enter destination, check-in/check-out dates, number of guests.
  - **Filters:** Price range, star rating, amenities.
  - **Results List:** Display hotels with booking options.
  - **Hotel Details:** More info about selected hotels.
  - **Booking Button:** Secure booking option.

### **4. To-Do Activities Manager**
- **Purpose:** Manage and track travel itineraries and activities.
- **Components:**
  - **Add Activity:** Create new activities with details and reminders.
  - **Edit/Delete Activity:** Modify or remove existing activities.
  - **Activity List:** Display upcoming and completed activities.
  - **Calendar Integration:** Sync with calendar apps.

### **5. Currency Converter**
- **Purpose:** Convert currencies and manage expenses.
- **Components:**
  - **Currency Selector:** Choose currencies to convert between.
  - **Amount Input:** Enter the amount to convert.
  - **Conversion Rates:** Display real-time rates and converted amount.
  - **Historical Data:** Option to view historical rates.

### **6. Live Location Sharing**
- **Purpose:** Share real-time location with contacts.
- **Components:**
  - **Enable Location:** Option to turn on location sharing.
  - **Share with Contacts:** Select contacts or generate a shareable link.
  - **Map View:** Display current location and sharing status.

### **7. Cost of Living Insights**
- **Purpose:** Provide information on the cost of living in various locations.
- **Components:**
  - **Cost Categories:** Display costs for food, transportation, lodging, etc.
  - **Location Selector:** Choose or search for different locations.
  - **Comparison Charts:** Visual representation of costs.

### **8. Nearby Popular Places**
- **Purpose:** Discover nearby attractions and popular spots.
- **Components:**
  - **Map View:** Show nearby places on a map.
  - **List View:** Display popular and hidden spots with descriptions.
  - **Search Bar:** Filter by type of place (restaurants, landmarks, etc.).



### **Navigation Structure**
- **Top Navigation Bar:** For quick access to main pages.
- **Side Navigation (Optional):** Can be used for secondary options or detailed navigation within pages.
- **Footer:** Include links for legal information and support.

**Design Tips:**
- Ensure **consistent styling** across all pages for a cohesive look.
- Use **clear labels and icons** to make navigation intuitive.
- **Responsive design**: Ensure navigation is user-friendly on both desktop and mobile devices.

This structure should help you create a well-organized and user-friendly app.
